shoulder Workout

Standing Dumbbell Shoulder Press

Target Muscles: Shoulders (Deltoids), Triceps

Stand with dumbbells at shoulder height, palms forward, press upward until arms extend fully, then lower back down.

Alternating Dumbbell Front Raise

Target Muscles: Shoulders (Anterior Deltoids)

Stand with dumbbells at your sides, raise one dumbbell in front to shoulder height, then lower and alternate arms.

Cable Lateral Raise

Target Muscles: Shoulders (Lateral Deltoids)

Instructions: Stand between two cable machines, grasp the handles with both hands, and raise your arms out to the sides until shoulder height. Slowly lower back to the starting position.

Dumbbell Lateral Raise

Target Muscles: Shoulders (Lateral Deltoids)

Set cables high, grasp handles, pull towards your face, squeeze shoulder blades, then slowly return to starting position.

Cross Cable Face Pull

Target Muscles: Upper Back (Rhomboids, Trapezius), Rear Deltoids

Set cables high, grasp handles, pull towards your face, squeeze shoulder blades, then slowly return to start.

Rear Delt Fly Machine

Target Muscles: Rear Deltoids, Upper Back

Instructions: Sit on the machine with your chest against the pad, grasp the handles, and pull them outward to shoulder height, then slowly return.
