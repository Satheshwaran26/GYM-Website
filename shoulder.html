Shoulder Workout

Overhead Press

Target Muscle: Shoulders

Instructions: Hold the barbell at shoulder height and press it overhead in a controlled motion.

Lateral Raise

Target Muscle: Lateral Deltoids

Instructions: Hold dumbbells by your sides and raise them to shoulder height with arms slightly bent.

Front Raise

Target Muscle: Front Deltoids

Instructions: Hold a weight in both hands and raise it to shoulder height with arms straight.
