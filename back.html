Back Workout

Deadlift

Target Muscle: Lower Back, Hamstrings

Instructions: Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, grasp the barbell, and lift by extending your hips and knees.

Pull-Ups

Target Muscle: Lats, Upper Back

Instructions: Hang from a bar with palms facing away, pull yourself up until your chin is above the bar.

Barbell Row

Target Muscle: Upper and Middle Back

Instructions: Bend at the waist, hold the barbell with palms facing down, and row towards your waist.
